Behind the Curtain

Editing decides rhythm. Lighting decides mood. Sound decides tension. When all three align, audiences forget they are watching a constructed reality — and that is the magic of the medium.

Mise-en-scène
Everything that appears within a frame — set, lighting, costume, blocking.
Diegetic Sound
Audio originating from within the story world, heard by the characters.
Match Cut
An edit that links two shots through visual or thematic continuity.
